# Product rules

## Product posture

- Internal panel for Interchile Clima commercial prospecting.
- Serious, corporate, and operational tone.
- Not a landing page and not a generic consumer SaaS aesthetic.

## Scope

- Store leads.
- Analyze leads commercially.
- Create, edit, approve, reject, and review email drafts.
- Register email sends.
- Manage opt-outs.

## Implementation expectations

- Prioritize clean architecture and maintainable code.
- Prefer clear workflows over automation that exceeds the MVP.
- Keep visible product language in Spanish.
- Keep database, file, variable, and type names in English.

## UI expectations

- Blue and gray corporate palette.
- Clean tables and clear forms.
- No tutorial banners, MVP disclaimers, or explanatory filler in the interface.
